Video Chat

To start video chat:

> The patient should visit the Enter room in a alloted slot and wait for the server to connect.

> The doctor should visit the particular shared link on the portal and join the patient in a alloted slot.

> Click on the camera icon in the lower right corner to view video in a full screen along with chat option

> The video chat to be held for a given time slot,after the chat ends the cost is included automatically in billing.
